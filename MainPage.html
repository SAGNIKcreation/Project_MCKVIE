About MCKV Institute of Engineering

MCKV Institute of Engineering is one of the top engineering colleges in West Bengal established almost two decades ago with a vision to foster the spirit of technological learning and its application through creating the right kind of opportunity and ambience for educating young minds in the digital era. The Institute has been accredited by NAAC with an 'A' Grade, and it is an Autonomous Institute affiliated to MAKAUT, WB and approved by AICTE.
